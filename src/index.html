What is a Fourier series?

$$s(x) = \sum_{n=-\infty}^\infty c_n\cdot e^{\frac{2\pi i nx}{P}}$$ where $$c_n = \frac{1}{P}\int_{-P/2}^{P/2} s(x)e^{-\frac{2\pi inx}{P}}\,dx$$

A Fourier series is an expansion a periodic function into a sum of trigonometric functions.
